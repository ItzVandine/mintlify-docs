# InfinitiCloud Partnership Terms & Conditions

## 1. Partnership Categories & Requirements

### Public SMP (Open to Public)

- Must maintain a dedicated InfinitiCloud channel in your Discord server.
- Must include an in-game NPC named **InfinitiCloud** at spawn.

### Personal Use (Private / Friends Only)

- Partners are required to pay **50% of the retail cost** for the selected server specifications.

### Content Creators (YouTube / Streaming)

- InfinitiCloud must be promoted in every video or livestream where our servers are featured or used.

---

# 2. Mandatory Integration Requirements

## For Public SMPs

### Discord Promotion

- A permanent and clearly visible Discord channel dedicated to InfinitiCloud must be maintained within your server.

### In-Game NPC

- An NPC named **InfinitiCloud** must be placed at the server spawn.
- When interacted with, the NPC must provide the official InfinitiCloud Discord invite link.

---

## For Content Creators

### Video Credits

- Every video or livestream featuring the server must contain either:
  - A verbal shoutout, or
  - A visible on-screen graphic stating:\
    **“Powered by InfinitiCloud”**

### Description Requirements

- Video descriptions must include:
  - A link to the InfinitiCloud website
  - A link to the official InfinitiCloud Discord server

---

# 3. Monitoring & Compliance

InfinitiCloud staff may conduct periodic audits to ensure partnership requirements are being followed. This may include:

- Joining your Minecraft server to verify NPC placement
- Checking Discord server visibility requirements
- Reviewing recent videos or livestreams for proper credits and promotion

---

# 4. Violation Policy & Partnership Termination

To ensure fair use of resources and maintain partnership quality, InfinitiCloud follows a **Three-Strike Policy**.

### First Warning

- A formal notice of non-compliance will be issued.

### Second Warning

- A final warning will be issued.
- Corrective action must be taken within **24 hours**.

### Third Violation

- The partnership will be terminated immediately.
- The hosted server may be suspended without further notice.

### Additional Note

- Terminated partnership slots may be reassigned to applicants on the waiting list.

---

# 5. Ethical Conduct

All partners represent the InfinitiCloud brand while participating in the partnership program.

InfinitiCloud reserves the right to terminate partnerships immediately, without prior warnings, if a partner is involved in:

- Illegal activities
- Severe toxicity or harassment
- Actions that damage InfinitiCloud’s reputation or community

---

# 6. Partnership Resource Policy

To maintain sustainable and high-performance hosting services:

- All new partner and sponsor servers are eligible for **up to 8GB RAM free of charge**.
- Servers requiring more than 8GB RAM may upgrade for a maintenance fee ranging from **₹100 – ₹200 INR** depending on requirements.

### Grandfathered Partnerships

- This policy only applies to partnerships formed after **April 4, 2026**.
- Existing partners prior to this date will retain their current allocations and benefits.
